\section{Error function properties}

Let us verify the value of \(\erf(\infty)\).  In the square that is

\begin{equation}\label{eqn:errorFunction:20}
\begin{aligned}
(\erf(\infty))^2
&=
\frac{4}{\pi}
\int_0^\infty e^{-s^2} ds
\int_0^\infty e^{-t^2} dt \\
&=
\frac{1}{\pi}
\int_{-\infty}^\infty e^{-s^2} ds
\int_{-\infty}^\infty e^{-t^2} dt \\
&=
\frac{1}{\pi} 2 \pi
\int_{0}^\infty e^{-r^2} r dr \\
&=
\int_\infty^0 d( e^{-r^2} ) \\
&= 1
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}
